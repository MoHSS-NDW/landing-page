How to get access

Dashboards and reports are confidential and can only be accessed by authorized personnel. Access to dashboards and reports should be formally requested through the Health Information Systems (HIS) Division, operating under the purview of the Health Information and Research Directorate (HIRD).
If you need access to these resources, please contact the HIS Division.
